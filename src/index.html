What is Wasatch Range Light Rail?

UTA TRAX SD100 #1021

Salt Lake City, UT - December 2025

We are a Northern Utah based HO Scale model railroad club, based around interurban, streetcar, trolley, and light rail transit.

Inspired primarily by the Utah Transit Authority (UTA) TRAX lightrail system in the Salt Lake Valley. Our aim is to create a modular layout that can be displayed at local events, and to share our love of model railroading with the community.

We our currently producing our first modules but intend to make available a system that's easily expandable and portable.

Our main focus is to create a system that's easy to use, affordable, and accessible for beginners, while still being enjoyable for experienced model railroaders.

We recognize portability is key too and intentionally have designed the system to be easily transportable and storable.

News

Latest Updates and Announcements

February 14, 2026

This project of mine has been in development for a while. The goal is to assemble a small and easy to move modular UTA TRAX layout to take to shows, among other places of course.

Currently, the first handful are in the process of being produced. I'm aiming to create a loop first, get programming done, then start getting these out for other people to build and enjoy.

These are being designed ahead of the upcoming Bachmann HO Scale S70US LRV's. The goal is to have a system in place the second they arrive.

I do have plans down the road to also make backdrops and structure facades for the modules too, based around the most popular elements of the UTA TRAX system.
