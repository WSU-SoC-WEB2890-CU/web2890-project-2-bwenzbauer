Member Info

Everything you need to know about joining the club

Who Can Join?

Membership is open to anyone interested in model railroading, light rail, streetcars, or transit modeling. No prior experience is required — beginners are welcome.

What We Model

  • HO Scale modular layouts
  • Light rail, interurban, and streetcar operations
  • Prototype-inspired Utah Transit Authority TRAX
  • DCC-based automation

Meetings

Meetings currently don't happen since this isn't a formal enough club yet.

Membership Dues

  • Individual: $0 / Year (were currently running free, because we're a small club)

Member Expectations

  • Follow club module and electrical standards
  • Assist with setup and teardown at public events
  • Respect and avoid handling equipment owned by other fellow members
  • Promote a welcoming and educational environment

How to Join

Send an email to wasatchrangelightrail@gmail.com about joining. Currently, there's no formal process or benefits to joining. We're just building a base to start from, but once we get modules going and enough of a group established, we'll start having meetings and attending events.
