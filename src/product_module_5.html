Product Overview

The 11" x 22" Station Module is designed specifically for HO scale light rail and streetcar layouts. Whether you're building a Salt Lake City–inspired layout or designing your own urban transit corridor, this module provides the perfect footprint for platform stations, overhead catenary systems, and detailed city scenes.
